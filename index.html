ABOUT ODT

Traders Under One Platform in theDeFi World.

One Dollor is a community-driven organization that is established to provide a one stop solution for all kind of traders under one platform in the DeFi world. We aim to empower our users with a comprehensive solution by providing them a platform that has benefits of a centralized exchange and beyond.

Multi Chain Dex

Connecting all isolated blockchains and establish a cross-chain asset exchange network, providing all necessary underlying support for the Defi ecosystem.

Never Ending Liquidity

One Dollor obtains its liquidity from a variety of exchanges & has the capacity to distribute a single trade transaction across multiple DEXs in order to obtain the most competitive prices.

Community Driven

Governed by its community, via forum discussions and, when pertinent, voting on proposals by the One Dollor Holders.
